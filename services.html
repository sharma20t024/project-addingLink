Samsung offers a wide range of products and services across various industries. Here are some of the key services provided by Samsung:

Consumer Electronics: Samsung is known for its extensive lineup of consumer electronics products, including smartphones, tablets, televisions, home appliances (refrigerators, washing machines, air conditioners, etc.), audio systems, and wearables.

Mobile Devices: Samsung is one of the leading manufacturers of smartphones and tablets. They offer a diverse range of devices, from flagship smartphones like the Samsung Galaxy S series and Note series to mid-range and budget-friendly options. Samsung also provides tablets with various screen sizes and functionalities.

Televisions and Home Theater Systems: Samsung produces a wide range of televisions, including LED, QLED, and UHD models with various screen sizes and features. They also offer home theater systems, soundbars, and other audiovisual products to enhance the viewing experience.

IT Solutions: Samsung provides a range of IT solutions for businesses, including mobile devices, tablets, laptops, monitors, and printers. They also offer software solutions, security features, and enterprise-level services to cater to the needs of organizations.

Home Appliances: Samsung manufactures a comprehensive range of home appliances, such as refrigerators, washing machines, dishwashers, air conditioners, vacuum cleaners, and kitchen appliances. These products often feature advanced technologies and smart capabilities.

Display Solutions: Samsung is a leading provider of display solutions for various applications. They produce high-quality displays for commercial signage, digital information boards, video walls, and large-format displays for businesses and organizations.

Healthcare: Samsung offers healthcare solutions that integrate technology and medical devices. These solutions include medical imaging equipment, digital radiography systems, ultrasound machines, and healthcare software for hospitals and clinics.

Smart Home and IoT: Samsung provides smart home solutions that enable users to control and monitor their home appliances and devices remotely. This includes smart hubs, smart locks, smart lights, and other IoT (Internet of Things) devices that can be interconnected for a connected home experience
